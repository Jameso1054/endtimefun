Some poems

11/4/21 i
Lo, On-High,
I spy in tow,
Within the sky,
A flaming bow.

My muscles crack,
My eyes the same,
For fore and back,
The angels aim.

11/4/21 iii
Warm sea of feeling,
Where swims and sails voice and string.
Through abyss you sing.

17/2/21
Matchmaking sisters kiss with sparks,
Nine titan daughters drown the arks,
Miss Have-Me-Never longs for you,
In your mind's drunken eye, this is true.

9/4/21
A washing tide laps o'er the shame,
Did I start, and need I go,
Heavy heart, and bogged down so,
Dolour applied is not the same,
A vortex sea, within a snail,
Clean and free, is that to fail?
A pointless ride, a horse so tame,
The rains decide the petty blame.
