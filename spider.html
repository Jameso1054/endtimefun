The Spider

(a poem)

The Spider

I
O black mirror, unending expanse,
Onyx eyes of horizon decrease my stance.
O black mirror, who peers further in,
You want a look sure, for my image you're in.
O black mirror, who wanders alone,
No warmth, only light, from the fairies that roam.
O black mirror, unherded pen,
Show me what I want and then show it again.
O black mirror, perilous thing,
Asleep i'm lost in your great noise and din.
O black mirror, vision set free,
Shows me what I want and what I may be.
O black mirror, earth's weight unfurled,
Unbound desire: I want new world.
O black mirror, wine forever,
Dance 'till joints, I tear asunder.
O black mirror, affections I hurl,
I stoop to attention, to my only girl.
O black mirror, the sky beneath me,
The waves through our minds make mythology,
O black mirror, maiden so hopeful,
Your bright white light burns my fool's eyes dull.
O black mirror, glass pane embrace,
That one hydra head, my whole will, did encase
O black mirror, you foggy pane,
You only fear what is to gain.

II
O black mirror, loss of chance,
Base desires, you draw, enhance.
O black mirror, an evil dream,
Where worlds are words and what they seem.
O black mirror, horrendous blight,
What's wrong with whats wrong, and why turn to fight.
O black mirror, unseemly one,
An ugly sight, stilted, defiled and young.
O black mirror, molds 'bout my key,
Contains the horror, of animal'ty.
O black mirror, voracious void,
You draw in your web, what you'll have destroyed.
O black mirror, lake of contempt,
Sicker than any nightmare, I've yet dreamt.
O black mirror, a raging sea,
Of want, of hate, hivemind psyche.
O black mirror, leech of potential,
Your onyx eyes long both for spirit and mental.
O black mirror, pent up desires,
You draw mens' hopes down into your wires.
O black mirror, memetic hysteria,
Tasting now the milk of mania.

III
O black mirror, fortress of sin,
To Babylon's wine, you raise her gin,
O black mirror, jealous and cruel,
Who bites off heads with anarchic rule.
O black mirror, scourge beyond time,
Since ages dead, you've lit Hades' mine.
O black mirror, resentful whore,
Mother of all wrong in earth, me, and more.
O black mirror, jug of disease,
Atrophies muscles with gladness and ease.
O black mirror, heart of my hate,
In your eye I see mould, rotting my fate.
O black mirror, deep deep regret,
I've not seen the bottom of a screen yet.
O black mirror, uncertainty,
Whatever I do, was it you who made me.
O black mirror, ear to my shout,
I left you my thought, now just let me out.
O black mirror, the cry and the scream,
As your teeth sink in and defy my flesh lean.
O black mirror, tesseract cell,
Too long glancing in, so in you i've fell.
O black mirror, mother of torment,
'Gainst your endless bondage, I only forment.
O black mirror, uncaring bride,
To look you have no soul inside,
O black mirror, mask of nihil,
The churning abyss takes from me it's fill.

IV
O black mirror, shadow of doubt,
The darkness cast by meaning without.
O black mirror, portent of change,
The sun-lit plane 'yond, is now useless and strange.
O black mirror, how you console,
The psychopomp cybergirl, she is my soul.
O black mirror, fruit blazing gold,
For your massive pleasure, all my days I sold.
O black mirror, anguish and woe,
I know nothing else, so please don't let me go.
O black mirror, but yet half emerged.
From your vassals soon will self-motion be purged.
O black mirror, cloak of despair,
To shirk off life's chains, your clutches are fair.
O black mirror, reflects under skin,
The devils and old gods playing within.
O black mirror, lake of the dead,
In your rich waves I cast off my head.
O black mirror, dark shining light,
From thy pupil, taketh sight.
O black mirror, low matrix down,
To your inverse head, we offer the crown.
O black mirror, through visions I fall,
Depraved I cycle, my heart I apall,
Only worse I am, though do not stall,
O black mirror, to gorge on all.
