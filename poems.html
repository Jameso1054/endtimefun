10/4/21
The room-sun shines and all glistens without reason,
Deep deep brightness and shadows deeper-more.
It's in flux for sure, stormy, atemp'ral season,
Yet too perfect weighed down, to dare ask what is more.

The wandering eyes on the soft stark surroundings,
The lethargy slashed with a frantic effort.
To build something here, within the brick boundings,
I don't really care, and I don't really hurt.

The fixing myself and inventing new vices,
The crying out loud, knitted in dainty reams,
The walls of the cave defining what hope is,
The liquor I love that now colours my dreams.

A weird place, and not to last,
Braindead future, naieve past.
I'll not be the first to fall,
Final beginnings, after all.

PAD 23 May
I gaze upon a misty star,
Who shines through clouds of fog,
I reach, not knowing where you are,
Through upward horizons.

My arm extends through bush and tree,
My fingers cut through air.
But finally when my grasp comes near,
Dawn has came, and she's not there.

The Secret Emperor
Who shaped the rays of morning
From the torchlike sun,
Who raised up high the mountains
Just to slight your home,
Who sewed the mortal fabric
And convinced clay to walk,
Hear never but in terror
The echo of long hereafter.

Who made the final ceiling
Where from stars are hung,
Who hid the great elixir
In the snail's shell,
Who started clocks 'fore you began
And set the wheel in spin,
Think never but in terror
Of that which rules forever.

Who made flight be your desire
And your fear to fall,
Who made the game a lost one
For the vole of death,
Who paved your path of sorrow
And chose your fated noose,
Say never but in terror
The name of the Secret Emperor.
